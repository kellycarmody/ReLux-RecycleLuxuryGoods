Process

1. Submit a form for a ReLux vendor to pick up your bulk items.
2. It will be shipped to a manufacturer who will remove the buttons, tags, and company markings.
3. The clothing will be ready to be purchased in bulk, either brandless style or to be recycled as insulation.
